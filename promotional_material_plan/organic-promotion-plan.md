# Organic Promotional Material Plan

## Weekly Promotion Rhythm

Week 1:

- Announce the 10 free website campaign on Hawke Tech Solutions Facebook and Instagram
- Post in local business groups where self-promotion is allowed
- Message existing contacts and past clients for referrals
- Ask Dhok Koon and Splash n' Dash if they are comfortable with being mentioned as example projects

Week 2:

- Share a short video explaining what makes a local business website convert
- Post examples of website sections: service page, contact section, mobile CTA, Google-friendly copy
- Share a reminder that applications are selected, not first come first served

Week 3:

- Publish a shortlist update if applications are coming in
- Share anonymised applicant themes: trades, hospitality, health, services
- Post a final call only if there is a real deadline

Week 4:

- Announce that applications are under review
- Contact selected businesses
- Ask non-selected but qualified businesses whether they want a paid quote or waitlist spot

## Local Channels

Post and outreach ideas:

- Wollongong business Facebook groups
- Illawarra small business groups
- Shellharbour and Kiama community groups where business posts are allowed
- LinkedIn post from Jesse's personal profile
- Instagram Reels and Stories
- Local networking groups
- Direct outreach to businesses with no website or visibly outdated sites

Group post template:

"Hi everyone, I am Jesse from Hawke Tech Solutions in Wollongong. I am selecting 10 Illawarra businesses for a free website design and build campaign. It is aimed at local businesses with no website, an outdated website, or a site that is not helping customers get in touch. The website design and build are free for selected businesses. Optional domain, hosting, paid tools, integrations, or ongoing support are quoted separately if needed. Apply here: https://hawketechsolutions.com/"

## Referral Message

"Hey, I am running a local campaign through Hawke Tech Solutions and choosing 10 Illawarra businesses for a free website design and build. If you know a business owner who needs a better website, could you send them this link? https://hawketechsolutions.com/"

## Follow-Up Templates

New applicant reply:

"Thanks for applying for the 10 free Illawarra websites campaign. I have received your details and will review them personally. If your business looks like a strong fit, I will reach out to organise a quick call."

Qualified applicant call invite:

"Your application looks like a good fit. Are you available for a 15-minute call this week to talk through your business, current website situation, and what the new site needs to achieve?"

Not selected but good fit:

"Thanks again for applying. I was not able to include every good business in the 10 free builds, but your project still looks worthwhile. If you would like, I can send through a simple paid website option or keep you on a waitlist for future campaigns."

## Promotional Assets to Create

- Square social graphic: "10 Free Websites for Illawarra Businesses"
- Story graphic: "Applications now open"
- Founder video: 20-30 seconds
- Website scroll recording: current landing page on mobile
- Proof graphic: "Built by Hawke Tech Solutions" with two example projects
- Local SEO explainer post: "What a business website needs to show up locally"

## Selection Criteria

Use a simple scoring sheet:

- Illawarra-based business
- Clear offer or service
- Website can realistically help acquisition
- Business can provide content quickly
- Good portfolio fit for Hawke Tech Solutions
- Owner is responsive and easy to collaborate with

## Campaign Closeout

When the 10 are selected:

- Update the landing page from "applications open" to "applications closed"
- Stop Meta ads
- Follow up with every applicant
- Create a waitlist for future paid or discounted projects
- Publish progress updates as the selected websites are built
