# Promotional Material and Meta Campaign Plan

This directory supports the Hawke Tech Solutions "10 free websites for Illawarra businesses" landing page campaign.

Files:

- `meta-campaign-plan.md` - Campaign setup, targeting, budget, lead form, measurement, and launch checklist.
- `ad-copy-and-creative.md` - Ready-to-use ad copy angles, creative briefs, and landing page message match.
- `organic-promotion-plan.md` - Local promotional material ideas for Facebook groups, email, partnerships, and referrals.

Primary campaign promise:

Hawke Tech Solutions is selecting 10 Illawarra businesses to receive a free website design and build. The offer should always clearly state that domain, hosting, paid tools, integrations, and ongoing maintenance may be separate costs if needed.
