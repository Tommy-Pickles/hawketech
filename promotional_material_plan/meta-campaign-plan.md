# Meta Campaign Plan - 10 Free Websites for Illawarra Businesses

## Campaign Goal

Generate qualified applications from Illawarra businesses that need a new or improved website. The campaign should prioritise lead quality over raw lead volume, because only 10 businesses will be selected.

Primary conversion:

- Landing page application form submission by email
- Backup conversion: phone call or SMS to Jesse

Recommended first test:

- Campaign objective: Leads
- Conversion locations: Website plus Instant Form if available in the ad account
- Optimisation: maximise leads at first, then move toward qualified leads once enough lead quality data exists
- Placements: Advantage+ placements unless a specific placement is wasting spend
- Geography: Illawarra, Wollongong, Shellharbour, Kiama, Port Kembla, Fairy Meadow, Corrimal, Dapto, Bulli, Thirroul, and nearby South Coast business areas

Sources checked:

- Meta says lead ads with forms can use instant forms and website forms for lead generation: https://www.facebook.com/business/ads/ad-objectives/lead-generation/lead-ads-with-forms
- Meta says Advantage+ leads campaigns use automated audience, placements, and campaign budget by default for leads campaigns where eligible: https://www.facebook.com/business/ads/meta-advantage-plus/leads
- Meta explains Business Tools such as Meta Pixel and Conversions API for measurement and optimisation: https://www.facebook.com/help/331509497253087/
- ACCC guidance says advertising must not be false or misleading, and "free" offers with conditions must be clear: https://www.accc.gov.au/consumers/advertising-promotions/false-or-misleading-claims

## Offer Guardrails

Use this line in ads and landing page copy:

"Free website design and build for 10 selected Illawarra businesses. Domain, hosting, paid tools, integrations, and ongoing support are quoted separately if required."

Avoid:

- "Guaranteed free website for everyone"
- "No cost ever"
- Fake countdowns or fabricated scarcity
- Implying Meta or local councils endorse the offer
- Calling out personal attributes like "Are you struggling?" or "Is your business failing?"

Use:

- "Illawarra businesses can apply"
- "10 selected businesses"
- "Free design and build"
- "Local website campaign"
- "Transparent optional costs"

## Campaign Structure

Campaign name:

`HTS | Free Website Giveaway | Illawarra | Leads`

Ad set 1:

- Name: `Illawarra Business Owners | Broad Local`
- Location: Illawarra and surrounding suburbs
- Age: 23-60
- Audience: Broad, with business/service interests only if needed
- Placement: Advantage+ placements
- Budget: start at AUD 20-40 per day for 7 days

Ad set 2:

- Name: `Engaged Local | Retargeting`
- Audience: Website visitors, Facebook/Instagram engagers, video viewers
- Budget: AUD 5-15 per day
- Run only after there is enough traffic or engagement

Ad set 3, optional:

- Name: `Local Service Categories`
- Audience: trades, hospitality, beauty, professional services, health/wellness, retail, creators
- Use only if broad targeting produces poor quality

## Budget and Testing

Phase 1 - Launch test, days 1-7:

- Spend: AUD 20-40 per day
- Run 3 creative angles
- Run both website lead and instant form if available
- Kill any ad with high spend and no meaningful lead activity after enough impressions

Phase 2 - Improve quality, days 8-14:

- Keep the best 1-2 ad concepts
- Add qualifying questions to instant forms
- Retarget landing page visitors and video viewers
- Move budget toward ads producing qualified businesses, not just cheap leads

Phase 3 - Close the 10 selections:

- Switch copy from "Applications open" to "Final review now open" only when true
- Increase urgency only if the application deadline is real
- Pause campaign once enough qualified applications exist

"Apply for one of 10 free website design and build spots for Illawarra businesses. Optional domain, hosting, paid tools, integrations, and ongoing support are quoted separately if needed."

Questions:

- Business name
- Business category
- Where do you operate?
- Do you currently have a website?
- What do you want the website to help you get?
- Website URL, if existing
- Best contact phone
- Best contact email

Qualification question:

"If selected, are you able to provide business details, images, service information, and feedback within 7 days?"

## Landing Page Message Match

Ad headline:

"10 Free Websites for Illawarra Businesses"

Landing page hero:

"10 free business websites for the Illawarra."

Why this matters:

The same phrase appears in the ad, landing page title, hero, and form. That makes the offer feel consistent and reduces drop-off after the click.
