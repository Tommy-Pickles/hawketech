# Ad Copy and Creative

## Creative Direction

Mood:

Local, practical, high-trust, and direct. The campaign should look like a serious local business opportunity, not a gimmick.

Visual system:

- Dark green/black background
- Warm yellow offer highlight
- Real screenshots or short screen recordings of finished business websites
- Local place language: Illawarra, Wollongong, Shellharbour, Kiama
- Clean CTA: Apply for a free website

Required disclosure in caption or creative:

"Free design and build for selected businesses. Optional domain, hosting, paid tools, integrations, and ongoing support quoted separately."

## Ad Angle 1 - Direct Offer

Primary text:

Illawarra business owners: Hawke Tech Solutions is selecting 10 local businesses for a free website design and build. If your current website is outdated, missing, or not bringing in enquiries, apply now.

Headline:

10 Free Websites for Illawarra Businesses

Description:

Free design and build for selected local businesses. Clear optional costs.

CTA:

Apply Now

Creative:

Static image with dark background, large "10 Free Websites", local website screenshot, and small text "Illawarra businesses only".

## Ad Angle 2 - Local Business Problem

Primary text:

Your website should make it easy for local customers to trust you, understand your offer, and get in touch. I am giving 10 Illawarra businesses a free website build to help them look professional online.

Headline:

Get a Better Business Website

Description:

Applications open for 10 local website builds.

CTA:

Learn More

Creative:

Before/after style without making exaggerated claims: left side "missing or outdated site", right side "clear mobile website". Keep it simple and do not imply guaranteed results.

## Ad Angle 3 - Founder-Led Local

Primary text:

I am Jesse from Hawke Tech Solutions in Wollongong. I build websites for local businesses, and I am choosing 10 Illawarra businesses for a free design and build campaign.

Headline:

Local Wollongong Web Design Offer

Description:

Apply for one of 10 free website builds.

CTA:

Apply Now

Creative:

Founder video or selfie-style reel. Script below.

## 20-Second Reel Script

"Hey, I am Jesse from Hawke Tech Solutions. I build websites for local businesses around Wollongong and the Illawarra. I am giving away 10 free website design and build spots to Illawarra businesses that need a better online presence. If you have no website, an old website, or a site that is not helping customers contact you, apply through the link. The build is free for selected businesses, and any optional domain, hosting, or ongoing costs are discussed upfront."

On-screen text:

- 10 free website builds
- Illawarra businesses
- Built by Hawke Tech Solutions
- Apply now

## 30-Second Reel Script

"A lot of local businesses are great at what they do, but their website does not show it. It might be slow, outdated, hard to use on mobile, or missing completely. Hawke Tech Solutions is selecting 10 Illawarra businesses for a free website design and build. The goal is simple: a clean, professional site that explains what you do, where you work, and how customers can get in touch. Apply on the website. I will personally review the applications and choose the 10 best fits."

## Creative Production Checklist

- 1 square static image, 1080 x 1080
- 1 vertical story/reel, 1080 x 1920
- 1 website scroll recording, 1080 x 1920
- 1 founder video, 20-30 seconds
- 1 proof creative showing Dhok Koon or Splash n' Dash as examples
- Add simple captions to all video
- Keep the free-offer conditions visible in caption or landing page

## A/B Test Matrix

Test 1:

- Direct offer static vs founder video

Test 2:

- "10 Free Websites" headline vs "Free Website Build for Illawarra Businesses"

Test 3:

- Website conversion landing page vs Meta Instant Form

Winning rule:

Do not choose the cheapest lead by default. Choose the ad that produces real Illawarra businesses with clear needs and fast response rates.
